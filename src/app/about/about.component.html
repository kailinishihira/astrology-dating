Fire Signs

The Trailblazers of the Zodiac

Ruled by the quick and active Fire element, these three zodiac signs are all about initiation, enthusiasm, courage, and moving forward. They shine a bright light on life and command attention wherever they go. Always ready to instigate others and start something new, Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius are the true trailblazers of the zodiac.

Aries

March 21 – April 19

Strengths: courageous, determined, confident, enthusiastic, optimistic, honest, passionate

Weaknesses: impatient, moody, short-tempered, impulsive, aggressive

Leo

July 23 – Aug. 22

Strengths: creative, passionate, generous, warm-hearted, cheerful, humorous

Weaknesses: arrogant, stubborn, self-centered, lazy, inflexible

Sagittarius

Nov. 22 – Dec. 21

Strengths: generous, idealistic, great sense of humor

Weaknesses: promises more than can deliver, very impatient, will say anything no matter how undiplomatic

Earth Signs

The Pragmatists of the Zodiac

Under the influence of the solid and practical Earth element, these three zodiac signs thrive on strength, determination, and practicality, and are at one with the physical world. They are focused and disciplined, eager to get the job done. Creating a true sense of foundation and security in the world, Taurus, Virgo, and Capricorn are the most pragmatic signs of the zodiac.

Taurus

April 20 – May 20

Strengths: reliable, patient, practical, devoted, responsible, stable

Weaknesses: stubborn, possessive, uncompromising

Virgo

Aug. 23 – Sept. 22

Strengths: loyal, analytical, kind, hardworking, practical

Weaknesses: shyness, worry, overly critical of self and others, all work and no play

Capricorn

Dec. 22 – Jan. 19

Strengths: responsible, disciplined, self-control, good managers

Weaknesses: know-it-all, unforgiving, condescending, expecting the worst

Air Signs

The Intellects of the Zodiac

Ruled by the clear and direct Air element, these three zodiac signs embrace truth, conversation, and ideas. They are communicators, mediators, and fighters of truth and justice. With sharp minds and a real sense of social awareness, Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius are the intellects of the zodiac.

Gemini

May 21 – June 20

Strengths: gentle, affectionate, curious, adaptable, ability to learn quickly and exchange ideas

Weaknesses: nervous, inconsistent, indecisive

Libra

Sept. 23 – Oct. 22

Strengths: cooperative, diplomatic, gracious, fair-minded, social

Weaknesses: indecisive, avoids confrontations, will carry a grudge, self-pity

Aquarius

Jan. 20 – Feb. 18

Strengths: progressive, original, independent, humanitarian

Weaknesses: runs from emotional expression, temperamental, uncompromising, aloof

Water Signs

The Intuitives of the Zodiac

With Water as their guiding force, these three zodiac signs have soft hearts, open minds, and old souls. They are lovers and nurturers, and live their lives with a deep understanding of what's happening around them -- the seen and the unseen. Sensitive, soulful, and connected, Cancer, Scorpio, and Pisces are the intuitives of the zodiac.

Cancer

June 21 – July 22

Strengths: tenacious, highly imaginative, loyal, emotional, sympathetic, persuasive

Weaknesses: moody, pessimistic, suspicious, manipulative, insecure

Scorpio

Oct. 23 – Nov. 21

Strengths: resourceful, brave, passionate, stubborn, a true friend

Weaknesses: distrusting, jealous, secretive, violent

Pisces

Feb. 19 – March 20

Strengths: compassionate, artistic, intuitive, gentle, wise, musical

Weaknesses: fearful, overly trusting, sad, desire to escape reality, can be a victim or a martyr

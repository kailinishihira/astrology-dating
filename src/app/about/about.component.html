Fire Signs

They shine a bright light on life and command attention wherever they go. Always ready to instigate others and start something new, Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius are the true trailblazers of the zodiac.

Weaknesses: courageous, determined, confident, ent arrogant, stubborn, self-centered, lazy, inflexible
